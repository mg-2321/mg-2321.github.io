I’m a second-year M.S. student in the Comuter Science department at the University of Washington. Currently, my research supervised by Dr. Ruta Desai focuses on agentic AI: planning and teaching language models when to ask vs. when to act, with lightweight grounding via a knowledge-augmented graph (KAG)for preference-based training of agents.

Additionally, I'm exploring on how to overcome memory context-window limits using short- and long-term retrieval caches, summarization/compression, and Reflexion-style episodic notes that are indexed and reused across tasks.

I’m open to research collaborations.
